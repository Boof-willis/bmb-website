Urban infrastructure development

‍

City work covers the projects that sit between a private developer and a municipality — road work, utility corridors, public spaces, civic buildings, and the mixed-use that ties downtowns and main streets together. Utah's growing fast, and the cities running these projects need contractors who can deliver on a public timeline without dropping the ball on quality.

We've worked across the Wasatch Back and Salt Lake Valley, so we know how the local planning departments, city engineers, and inspectors operate. We pull permits, coordinate with agencies, and run the public-facing communication so the city gets the project they were promised and nobody's surprised at the ribbon cutting.
